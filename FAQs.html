Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Perfect Fit!

What is Perfect Fit?
Perfect Fit is a personalized fitting solution that uses advanced technology to help you find the perfect size for your clothing needs.
How do I create my avatar?
To create your avatar, simply navigate to the avatar section in the app, select 'Create Avatar', and follow the prompts to customize your appearance.
How does the sizing comparison work?
The sizing comparison tool allows you to compare your measurements against our size chart to find the best fit for you based on your unique dimensions.
How can I reset my password?
To reset your password, click on the 'Forgot Password?' link on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions in the email you receive.
What if I have a problem with my order?
If you encounter any issues with your order, please contact our customer support team via email or chat for assistance.
How can I contact customer support?
You can reach our customer support team through the 'Contact Us' section of our website, via email, or through our live chat feature.
Is there a mobile version of the app?
Yes, you can download it from the App Store or Google Play.
For further assistance, please contact our support team at support@perfectfit.com
